About Adventure Saathi

Adventure Saathi was founded by Shailza, a mountain lover and trekking enthusiast who believes the Himalayas are more than just destinations — they are experiences that transform you. From the snowy trails of Kedarkantha to the high passes of Himachal, Adventure Saathi is built to bring authentic trekking experiences to explorers who seek adventure beyond ordinary travel. Our mission is simple — to make the mountains accessible, safe, and unforgettable for every adventurer.
